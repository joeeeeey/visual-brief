# Mode Selection

Choose a mode from the reader's next action, not from whether the material came
from a browser or an API.

| Reader question | Mode | Required outcome |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "Why should we not use this path?" | `evidence-reply` | conclusion, minimum decisive claims, sources, boundaries |
| "How do I finish this task in the system?" | `visual-procedure` | ordered actions, adjacent screenshots, expected states, success condition |
| "How do I explain this technical idea publicly?" | `public-explainer` | clear thesis, explanatory/evidence visuals, sources, channel drafts |

When one long-form deliverable includes a decision and an operation, keep one
source manifest and label the sections clearly in `deliverable.md`. Do not
create duplicate evidence just to force content into three separate modes.
